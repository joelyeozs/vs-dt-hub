🧠 DT Mindsets

Before you use the tools, adopt the mindset. These five shifts make all the difference.

👥

Human-Centred

Design for real people, not assumptions. Empathy before everything.

🧪

Bias to Action

A rough prototype today beats a perfect plan on paper. Make it tangible.

🌱

Embrace Ambiguity

Unclear problems are opportunities. Sit with not-knowing — for now.

🔁

Learn from Failure

Fail fast, learn faster. Every setback is data for your next iteration.

🤝

Radical Collaboration

Diverse perspectives make better solutions. Seek out people unlike you.

🚧

What's Blocking You?

Identify your block, then flip it into a mindset shift.

Common Blocks → Mindset Flip

Block"I don't know enough about the user." → Go back to Empathise. One more conversation changes everything.
Block"My idea isn't good enough." → You need more ideas, not better ones. Ideate more wildly first.
Block"The problem is too big." → Narrow your focus. Who is the one person you're designing for today?
Block"My prototype looks terrible." → Perfect. Low fidelity = low attachment = more honest feedback.
Block"We disagree as a group." → Disagreement is a feature. Try both approaches and test.
